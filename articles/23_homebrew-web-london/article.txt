Text:
For the first time in a long long while London Homebrew Website Club is happening!

<a href="http://tantek.com">Tantek</a> was in town so luckily made it by. Along with him we had <a href="https://tommorris.org">Tom Morris</a> and <a href="https://barryfrost.com">Barry Frost</a> attending.

We've been discussing a range of topics/technologies for our websites including events publication, linking up various features via Bridgy, photo tagging/syndicating and live displaying what music you're listening to.

We're planning to make this a monthly to begin with so if you're in London next month and have a website feature to work on, show or even just to offer or get some help â€“ come along!
